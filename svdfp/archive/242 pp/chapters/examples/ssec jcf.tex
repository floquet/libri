\subsection{Jordan blocks}

The generic Jordan block.
\begin{equation}
  \A{} =
  \mat{cccc}{
  \lambda & 1  \\
    & \ddots & \ddots \\
    & & \ddots & 1 \\
    & & &  \lambda
   }
\end{equation}
