\section{Adding noise}
At each step, pick a random pixel $p$ and add noise uniformly distributed $\eps\in\brac{-0.05,0.05}$:
%
\begin{equation}
  \A{}_{p} \to \A{}_{p} + \eps
\end{equation}
%
Eventually the noise overwhelms and destroy the image.
